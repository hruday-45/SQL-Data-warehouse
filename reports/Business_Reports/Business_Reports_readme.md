# Business Reports - Olist Brazil

This folder contains SQL reports analyzing revenue, customers, and sales trends at the business level.

## Reports

### 01. Total & Monthly Revenue
- **Goal:** Calculate overall revenue, average order value, and monthly revenue trends
- **SQL File:** `01_total_and_monthly_revenue.sql`
- **Insights:** December had the highest revenue. Revenue shows steady growth.

### 02. Top Revenue Categories
- **Goal:** Identify product categories contributing the most revenue and profit
- **SQL File:** `02_top_revenue_categories.sql`
- **Insights:** Electronics and Home appliances contribute 60% of revenue.

### 03. Top Customers by Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **Goal:** Identify top 10% customers by total spend
- **SQL File:** `03_top_customers_by_LTV.sql`
- **Insights:** Top customers are responsible for 40% of total revenue.

### 04. Customer Repeat Purchase Analysis
- **Goal:** Measure percentage of repeat buyers and retention trends
- **SQL File:** `04_customer_repeat_purchase.sql`
- **Insights:** 25% of customers are repeat buyers within 6 months.
